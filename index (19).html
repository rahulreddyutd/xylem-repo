Project 01 — New Product Development

Smart Leak Detection Sensor Launch

Municipal water utilities lose 20–30% of treated water to undetected pipe leaks. Existing detection is slow and labor-intensive. The opportunity: a low-cost IoT sensor with real-time alerts and a SaaS analytics layer.

Deliverable A

VOC Research Report

Interviews with 10 utility managers. Top 5 unmet needs mapped in a severity × frequency matrix. Direct input into requirements management tool.

Deliverable B

3-Phase Product Roadmap

MVP → Analytics Dashboard → API Integrations. Milestones, dependencies, and release criteria for each Stage Gate review.

Deliverable C

Business Case & P&L

3-year revenue and margin model. Hardware + SaaS pricing strategy. Break-even analysis and cost targets aligned with operations and finance.

Deliverable D

GTM Launch Plan

Target segments, channel partner strategy, beta program design, and sales enablement kit for regional teams.

Project 02 — Portfolio Strategy

80/20 SKU Rationalization

A pump product line grew to 140 SKUs but 80% of revenue comes from just 28 of them. Excess complexity drives up inventory carrying costs, production inefficiency, and sales confusion across regions.

Deliverable A

Pareto Analysis

Revenue + margin by SKU. Vital few vs trivial many. Waterfall chart visualization with clear decision tiers for each SKU group.

Deliverable B

Competitor Matrix

3-competitor map across price, feature coverage, and channel. Whitespace and threat analysis to inform retention decisions.

Deliverable C

Rationalization Plan

40 SKUs to sunset, 12 to bundle, 8 platform SKUs to invest in. Customer migration path and sales team transition playbook.

Deliverable D

Executive Presentation

10-slide deck with data story, risk/benefit analysis, implementation roadmap, and 3-year forecast impact for leadership review.

Project 03 — Data & Analytics

Product Performance Dashboard

Regional sales teams lack visibility into which products are gaining or losing market share. Pricing decisions are made on gut feel. A structured tracker is needed to enable data-driven product and pricing decisions.

Deliverable A

KPI Framework

12 core metrics across revenue, margin, win rate, NPS, and market share. Owners, cadence, and RAG status definitions per metric.

Deliverable B

Market Trend Report

Water scarcity trends, regulatory shifts, and smart infrastructure spending analysis. Each trend linked to a specific product opportunity.

Deliverable C

Pricing Analysis

Price-to-value map vs 3 competitors. Recommended price corridor adjustments by region and channel with risk assessment.

Deliverable D

Interactive Dashboard Mockup

Product scorecard with real vs target KPIs, trend sparklines, and early warning flags for underperforming SKUs by region.
